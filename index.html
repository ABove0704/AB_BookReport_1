What was AOL?

Steve Case brought AOL to the World Wide Web. Case wanted to have a new online service for users to have multiple opinions of webbing, checking e-mails, and even playing games on their websites. AOL brought millions of users the opinion to check e-mails from other users. This concept made it easier for users to send messages through the web rather than sending people a letter that will take days or weeks to receive. This new invention made communication earlier for people trying to send a message out as quickly as possible.
